### Decisions inherited from brief (carried into PRD, not re-litigated)
- Two North-Star metrics: First-pass payroll accuracy (≥99.5% v1) + Time-to-First-Payroll (≤3 business days from data-complete). Clock starts at **data-complete**, not sign-up.
- Prevented-error rate is the demo hero stat / proof-of-thesis (secondary metric, not a sold SLA).
- Employee self-service portal is **in** v1 but **read-only** (protects onboarding speed).
- India-only is a deliberate moat — multi-country/multi-currency explicitly out.
- v1 integrations pinned (not "most common"): eSSL, ZKTeco, Biomax, Matrix COSEC (biometric); Tally, Zoho Books, QuickBooks (finance); bank portals (disbursement); CSV employee-master import (HRMS).
- Compliance engine must be rules-versioned + state-aware — no hard-coded 2025 snapshot.
